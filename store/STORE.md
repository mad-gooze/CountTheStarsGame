## English (en)

**Short description** `[50]`
Relax and count glowing stars in a quiet night sky

**SEO description** `[115]`
Count the stars in a calm, meditative night-sky game. Tap each new star, beat your best score and unwind. Play now!

**About the game** `[626]`
Count The Stars is a calm, meditative arcade game about a quiet night sky. Stars light up one by one and your only job is to count them by tapping each new one as it appears. Stay focused: tap an old star by mistake and you lose one of your three chances. Every correct tap adds a star to your count and gently lights up the sky. There is no timer and no pressure, just soft ambient music, glowing stars and your own growing record. Beat your personal best, relax after a long day, and see how many stars you can count. Plays instantly in your browser on desktop and mobile, with full support for English, Russian and Turkish.

**How to play** `[503]`
Watch the night sky: a new glowing star appears with every round. Tap or click the newest star to count it; your score goes up and another star lights up. Be careful not to tap stars you have already counted. Each wrong tap costs one of your three chances, and the game ends when they run out. On a phone or tablet, tap the stars with your finger; on a computer, click them with the mouse. Use the speaker icon to mute or unmute the music. Try to count as many stars as you can and beat your best score.
